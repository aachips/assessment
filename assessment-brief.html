Website Plan: AAChips.co/AdultAssessment

By April A Cyr. January 8, 2023. Sample Formatting

Did you know?

  • According to the CDC, more than five million adults in the U.S. have Autism Spectrum Disorder(ASD). Close to ten million have Attention Deficit Hyperactivity Disorder(ADHD).[1]
  • Autistic adults over 18 struggle to find services and get the support they need.[2] Diagnostic services for adults can cost thousands of dollars in out-of-pocket costs and have years-long waiting lists.[3]
  • Public K12 Schooling does not adequately prepare young people for the everyday challenges of adult life.[4]

What is the Assessment for Adult Competences(AAC)?

AAC is an interactive non-diagnostic assessment tool that checks for commonly shared problems among adults on the Autism and ADHD spectrums.

Assessment results have no legal or medical standing, but may be helpful for planning goals and service implementation. Each question is carefully worded to be unambiguous and designed to measure Frequency, Severity, and Hypo-/Hyper- Ranging.

Each question has instructions, additional information, and fun vector graphics. Universal Accessibility is our goal, and to get there, we are accepting all types of User Feedback.

Overall Goals

  1. Improve the outcomes and quality of life for young adults (legislation referenced is US-Centric).
  2. Catalog and index upwards of 200 unique experience markers among six categories of adult life.
  3. Meaningfully connect vulnerable adults to services, resources, and online peer communities.
  4. Reduce stigmas of common struggles for young adults with evidence-based information.
  5. Bewilder our digital technologies professors by doing the tutorial assignment wrong.
  6. Shrink the barriers to access for care and support between diagnosed and undiagnosed adults.
  7. Implement a peer-to-peer forum board over Reddit.com to specifically discuss experiences and adaptive coping skills.

Specific Features

This will include the following features:

  • HTML, CSS, and PHP based coding for style, interactivity, and usability
  • Conducive rainbow color scheming for sections
  • Automated PDF Report function
  • Scoring system based on Frequency(1-6), Severity(1-6), and Hypo-/Hyper-Ranging(-1, 1). (-36, 36)
  • Fun vector graphics on each question to maintain attention
  • Session saving and ability to assess one out of the six sections at a time. Metric and user information tracking for research purposes while maintaining a simple and transparent privacy policy
  • Readability, Scanability, Usability, Accessibility, Scalability
  • Approximately 200 questions with two half-pages of compiled research and anecdotal information about different struggles and adult experiences
  • Patreon and Kofi donation based feed to give April money
  • Online store for Laminate Communication Boards
  • Adhere to AA grade or above Web Content Accessibility Guidelines

Target Audiences

  1. The primary target audience for AAChips.co/AdultAssessment are young adults and their families and loved ones. Especially when ADHD and / or Autism Spectrum Developmental Delays are in play.
  2. The secondary target audience is social workers, agencies, and human services professionals who work with young adults, who want a tool to help map out age-appropriate goals for autonomy, independence, and supported decision making of their clients.
  3. The tertiary target audience is clinical researchers who are interested in simpler, more effective assessment models of mapping the common life challenges of young adults with disabilities.
